Citizenship Act

AST-2026-03

For Crown and People

ARTICLE I - ESTABLISHMENT OF CITIZENSHIP

Section 1: Definition

Citizenship of the Principality of Astoria shall constitute formal membership in the Principality and allegiance to the Crown.

Section 2: Authority

Citizenship shall be granted, recognized, or revoked under the authority of the Princess Regnant.

ARTICLE II - OBTAINING CITIZENSHIP

Section 1: By Application

Any person may apply for citizenship to the Principality of Astoria.

Such applications shall be reviewed and approved by the Ministry of the Interior.

Section 2: By Proclamation

The Princess Regnant may grant citizenship directly to any person at her discretion.

Section 3: By Qualification for Office

If a non-citizen is uniquely qualified for a position requiring citizenship, and is otherwise eligible for such position, their citizenship application may be immediately submitted for expedited review.

Citizenship may be granted by the Princess Regnant to permit appointment to such office.

ARTICLE III - RIGHTS OF CITIZENS

Section 1: General Rights

Citizens shall enjoy all rights guaranteed under the Constitution and laws of the Principality of Astoria.

Section 2: Political Rights

Only citizens of the Principality of Astoria shall have the right to:

  • Vote in elections
  • Stand as a candidate for elected office
  • Hold positions of crucial authority within the Government

ARTICLE IV - STATUS OF NON-CITIZENS

Section 1: General Status

Non-citizens may reside within and participate in the Principality of Astoria.

Section 2: Rights and Responsibilities

Non-citizens shall enjoy most rights and responsibilities, except where restricted by law.

Non-citizens shall not exercise political rights reserved for citizens.

ARTICLE V - REVOCATION OF CITIZENSHIP

Section 1: By Authority

The Princess Regnant may revoke the citizenship of any person at her discretion.

Section 2: For Grave Crimes

Citizenship may be revoked for acts of:

  • Treason
  • Grave crimes against the Principality
  • Actions deemed severely harmful to the nation

Such revocation shall be carried out under the authority of the Princess Regnant.

ARTICLE VI - RECORD OF CITIZENSHIP

The Government shall maintain an official registry of citizens of the Principality of Astoria.

ARTICLE VII - ALLEGIANCE

All citizens shall owe allegiance to:

  • The Principality of Astoria
  • The Constitution
  • The Princess Regnant

ARTICLE VIII - FINAL AUTHORITY

Nothing in this Act shall limit the authority of the Princess Regnant over matters of citizenship.

ARTICLE IX - EFFECTIVE DATE

This act shall take effect immediately upon its lawful passage and publication.
