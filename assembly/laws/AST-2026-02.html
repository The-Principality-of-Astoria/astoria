ARTICLE I - ESTABLISHMENT OF THE MINISTRIES

Section 1: Creation

The executive administration of the Principality of Astoria shall be conducted through Ministries established under the authority of the Princess Regnant.

Section 2: Purpose

The Ministries shall exist to administer the Principality, to carry out the functions of government, and to execute the law in accordance with the Constitution and the will of the Crown.

Section 3: The Crown

All authority exercised by the Ministries derives from the Princess Regnant.

Nothing in this Act shall limit or restrict the absolute authority of the Princess Regnant.

ARTICLE II - AUTHORITY OF THE PRINCESS REGNANT

The Princess Regnant shall:

  • Appoint all Ministers
  • Remove any Minister at her discretion
  • Delegate, modify, revoke, or assume the authority of any Ministry at any time
  • Remain Commander-in-Chief of the Royal Astorian Armed Forces

ARTICLE III - THE CHIEF MINISTER

Section 1: Establishment

There shall exist an office designated the Chief Minister of Astoria.

Section 2: Authority and Duty

The Chief Minister shall:

  • Coordinate the operation of the Ministries
  • Oversee executive administration
  • Advise the Princess Regnant
  • Recommend ministerial appointments

The Chief Minister shall serve at the pleasure of the Princess Regnant.

ARTICLE IV - THE MINISTRY OF DEFENSE

Section 1: Establishment

There shall exist a Ministry of Defense.

Section 2: Authority

The Ministry of Defense shall:

  • Administer the Royal Astorian Armed Forces
  • Maintain military readiness
  • Conduct military planning and logistical coordination
  • Coordinate between the Armed Forces and the Government

The Ministry of Defense shall not supersede the authority of the Princess Regnant as Commander-in-Chief.

ARTICLE V - THE MINISTRY OF THE INTERIOR

Section 1: Establishment

There shall exist a Ministry of the Interior.

Section 2: Authority

The Ministry of the Interior shall:

  • Administer the police force
  • Administer the prison system
  • Maintain public safety
  • Maintain internal security
  • Administer the immigration system

The Ministry of the Interior shall enforce law but shall not exercise judicial authority.

ARTICLE VI - THE MINISTRY OF THE TREASURY

Section 1: Establishment

There shall exist a Ministry of the Treasury.

Section 2: Authority

The Ministry of the Treasury shall:

  • Administer the national treasury
  • Draft budgetary plans
  • Monitor government expenditure
  • Receive financial requests from Ministries
  • Carry out duties established under law

ARTICLE VII - THE MINISTRY OF JUSTICE

Section 1: Establishment

There shall exist a Ministry of Justice

Section 2: Authority

The Ministry of Justice shall:

  • Conduct criminal prosecution
  • Administer government legal processes
  • Assist in the development of law
  • Advise the Government on legal matters
  • Ensure compliance with civil rights

The Ministry of Justice shall not exercise judicial authority. Judicial authority shall remain solely with the courts of the Principality, subject to the sovereign authority of the Princess Regnant.

ARTICLE VIII - THE MINISTRY OF FOREIGN RELATIONS

Section 1: Establishment

There shall exist a Ministry of Foreign Relations.

Section 2: Authority

The Ministry of Foreign Relations shall:

  • Conduct diplomacy
  • Represent the Principality in foreign affairs
  • Maintain relations with foreign entities
  • Execute foreign policy as directed by the Princess Regnant

ARTICLE IX - MINISTERIAL STRUCTURE

Each Ministry may establish subordinate offices, departments, and agencies as necessary.

Such subordinate bodies shall remain under the authority of the Minister and the Princess Regnant.

ARTICLE X - RELATIONSHIP TO THE CROWN

All Ministries shall serve the Crown and the Principality.

The Ministries shall carry out lawful directives of the Princess Regnant.

No vacancy in any Ministry shall impair the authority of the Princess Regnant.

The Princess Regnant may assume the functions of any Ministry.

ARTICLE XI - EFFECTIVE DATE

This Act shall take effect immediately upon approval by the Princess Regnant.
