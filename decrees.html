RD-2026-001 - Proclamation of the Constitution (Issued: 25 February 2026) - In Force

Summary: To proclaim the establishment of the Principality of Astoria and its Constitution.

Text:

By the authority vested in the Crown, and in recognition of the enduring sovereignty, unity, and future of the Principality.

I, Lillian Rose, Her Sovereign Highness, the Princess Regnant of the Principality of Astoria, do hereby proclaim and establish this Constitution as the supreme governing framework of the Principality of Astoria.

This Constitution shall define the structure, function, and continuity of the Government of Astoria, establish the institutions of the state, affirm the rights and duties of its citizens, and secure the perpetual sovereignty of the Crown.

From this day forward, all offices, institutions and authorities of Astoria shall operate in accordance with this Constitution, under the supreme authority of the Princess Regnant.

The Grand Assembly of Astoria shall convene under its provisions.

The Courts of Astoria shall administer justice under its provisions.

The Armed Forces of Astoria shall defend the Principality under its provisions.

The Constitution shall take full force and effect immediately upon this proclamation.

Let it be known to all citizens of Astoria, and to all who may observe its rise, that the Principality stands established in continuity, sovereignty, and purpose.

For All Time, For All People, Always.

Issued under the Crown this 25th day of February, in the year 2026

Signed,

Her Sovereign Highness

Lillian Iris Rose

Princess Regnant of Astoria
