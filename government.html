The Government of the Principality of Astoria operates under the sovereign authority of the Princess Regnant. Executive, legislative, and judicial institutions administer the affairs of the state in accordance with the Constitution and the lawful will of the Crown.

The Crown

Her Sovereign Highness, Lillian Iris Rose
Princess Regnant of Astoria

Date of Ascension: 25 February 2026

The Princess Regnant is the sovereign ruler of the Principality of Astoria. She serves as Head of State, Head of Government, and Commander-in-Chief of the Royal Astorian Armed Forces.

The Executive Government

Executive authority derives from the Princess Regnant and is administered through the Ministries of the Principality. The Chief Minister coordinates the operation of the Ministries and supports the Crown in the execution of government policy.

The Grand Assembly of Astoria

The Grand Assembly is the legislative body of the Principality of Astoria and consists of eighteen Members. The Assembly drafts, debates, and passes legislation and budget measures under constitutional procedure, with laws presented to the Princess Regnant for certification or denial.

The Judiciary

The High Court of Astoria is the highest judicial authority of the Principality. It interprets the Constitution and the laws of Astoria, and may review legislative acts for constitutional compliance in accordance with law.

The Royal Astorian Armed Forces

The Royal Astorian Armed Forces are the uniformed services of the Principality of Astoria, charged with defense of the nation, its citizens, and the Crown. The Princess Regnant serves as Commander-in-Chief.

Office of the Electorate

The Office of the Electorate administers citizen polling and elections of the Principality. It is constitutionally established as an independent office, separate from both the Executive Government and the Grand Assembly.
