Welcome to Astoria

The Principality of Astoria is a sovereign state under the rule of Her Sovereign Highness, the Princess Regnant.

This site serves as the official portal of the Crown, the Government, and the People of Astoria.

The Crown

The Princess Regnant serves as the supreme authority of Astoria, ensuring the stability, continuity, and prosperity of the Principality.

The Government

The Grand Assembly and Ministries administer the daily function of the state under the sovereignty of the Crown.
